```markdown

### Pricing
| Tier | Price | Features |
|------|-------|----------|
| Free | $0 | [Basic features] |
| Pro | $X/mo | [Pro features] |
| Enterprise | Custom | [Enterprise features] |

**Pricing Strategy:** [Premium / Value / Freemium / etc.]

2. **[Strength 2]:** [Details and impact]
3. **[Strength 3]:** [Details and impact]

### Weaknesses
1. **[Weakness 1]:** [Details and opportunity]

### Threat Level: [High / Medium / Low]
**Rationale:** [Why this threat level]
```
