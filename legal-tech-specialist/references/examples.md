```markdown

| Term | Value | Notes |
|------|-------|-------|

**Our Obligations:**
- [ ] [Obligation 1]
- [ ] [Obligation 2]
- [ ] [Obligation 3]

**Their Obligations:**
- [ ] [Obligation 1]
- [ ] [Obligation 2]
- [ ] [Obligation 3]

| Risk | Severity | Clause | Recommendation |
|------|----------|--------|----------------|
| [Risk 1] | High | §5.2 | Negotiate |
| [Risk 2] | Medium | §8.1 | Accept with note |
| [Risk 3] | Low | §12.4 | Accept |

2. **§7.1 IP:** Clarify ownership of deliverables
3. **§9.3 Termination:** Add termination for convenience

**Overall Assessment:** [Proceed / Negotiate / Decline]
**Requires Legal Review:** [Yes - for items X, Y, Z]
```

## Example 3

```markdown
## Non-Disclosure Agreement Structure

### Essential Elements

1. **Parties**
   - Full legal names
   - Business addresses
   - Authority to sign

2. **Definitions**
   - "Confidential Information"
   - "Disclosing Party" / "Receiving Party"
   - "Purpose"

3. **Scope of Confidentiality**
   - What's covered
   - What's excluded
   - Marking requirements

4. **Obligations**
   - Non-disclosure
   - Non-use beyond purpose
   - Protection standards
   - Employee/contractor flow-down

5. **Term**
   - Agreement duration
   - Survival period
   - Return/destruction

6. **Exceptions**
   - Public domain
   - Prior knowledge
   - Independent development
   - Legal compulsion

7. **Remedies**
   - Injunctive relief
   - Damages

8. **General Provisions**
   - Governing law
   - Dispute resolution
   - Amendment process
   - Entire agreement
```

## Example 4

```markdown
## Terms of Service Framework

### Section Structure

1. **Introduction**
   - Service description
   - Acceptance of terms
   - Age requirements

2. **Account Terms**
   - Registration requirements
   - Account security
   - Account termination

3. **Use of Service**
   - Permitted use
   - Prohibited activities
   - User content

4. **Intellectual Property**
   - Service IP
   - User content license
   - Feedback

5. **Payment Terms** (if applicable)
   - Pricing
   - Billing
   - Refunds
   - Taxes

6. **Privacy**
   - Reference to Privacy Policy
   - Data handling summary

7. **Third-Party Services**
   - Integrations
   - Links
   - Disclaimers

8. **Disclaimers & Limitations**
   - Warranty disclaimers
   - Liability limitations
   - Indemnification

9. **Termination**
   - User termination
   - Service termination
   - Effect of termination

10. **Dispute Resolution**
    - Informal resolution
    - Arbitration (if applicable)
    - Governing law

11. **Changes to Terms**
    - Modification process
    - Notice requirements

12. **Contact Information**
```

## Example 5

```markdown
## Contract Comparison Analysis

**Contracts Compared:**
- Contract A: [Vendor A Agreement]
- Contract B: [Vendor B Agreement]

### Key Terms Comparison

| Term | Contract A | Contract B | Preference |
|------|------------|------------|------------|
| Term Length | 12 months | 24 months | A |
| Auto-Renewal | Yes (30 day notice) | No | B |
| Termination for Convenience | Yes | No | A |
| Liability Cap | 12 months fees | Unlimited | A |
| SLA | 99.9% | 99.5% | A |
| Price | $1,000/mo | $800/mo | B |
| Support | 24/7 | Business hours | A |
| Data Location | US only | Flexible | Neutral |
| IP Ownership | Customer owns | Shared | A |

### Risk Comparison

| Risk Area | Contract A | Contract B |
|-----------|------------|------------|
| Financial Risk | Medium | Low |
| Operational Risk | Low | Medium |
| Legal Risk | Low | High |
| Vendor Lock-in | Low | High |

### Recommendation

**Preferred Contract:** A

**Rationale:**
- Better liability protection
- More flexibility
- Lower overall risk

**If Choosing B:**
- Negotiate liability cap
- Add termination for convenience
- Review with counsel
```
